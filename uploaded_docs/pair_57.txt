https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=9484db4f8bac85957b56334fb18586c449a1afc776 How to setup Screen Connect for Android and Samsung Devices Remote Screen Connect Guide (Android) Open the Google Play Store for Android and search for Connect Wise Control and Download the application Open your browser and go to this website "https://remote.csusb.edu/" and enter access code to join a session Click on the blue and white arrow and your phone will join your session. You will be prompted to “Consent to control device” click agree. Click on Start now to start recording or casting with control and click on yes to allow your host to control your device
