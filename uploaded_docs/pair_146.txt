You can access your OneDrive files from anywhere. You can also choose to share files with others within and outside the University. To access OneDrive using your CSUSB account either install one of the following clients or visit the website at: Csusb Sharepoint Windows 10 Windows 8 and 8.1 devices (Windows App) Mac OSX Android (Phone or Tablet) Apple iOS (iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch) Windows Phone (Versions 7, 7.5, and 8) Getting Help If you need assistance accessing or setting up your Office 365 account, please contact the Technology Support Center by Phone: (909)537-7677, Email: support@csusb.edu, or visiting us on the Web: Support Center.
