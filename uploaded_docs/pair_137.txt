https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=946688809220c7036a396940bc8169e623c4b9b5d5 How to install Office 365 If you already have the software installed but are having issues you may need to uninstall the software and then reinstall. To uninstall office please go here: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/uninstall-office-from-a-pc-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8 To download please go to: https://www.csusb.edu/its/software/student-software and scroll down to the Microsoft Office 365 and click on Access the Office 365 Portal Type in your CoyoteID number including @csusb.edu (Please note that coyote is NOT included.) Click Next or if prompted for a password, as soon as you begin to enter your password, you will be redirected to our campus login page. Enter your credentials. Username = myCoyote 9 digit ID# Password = myCoyote Password When you login, you will be taken to a page where you will find Office, click “Install Office” on the right. This will begin the download of the Microsoft Office product. After downloading just follow the on screen prompts and sign into the software with your CoyoteID#@csusb.edu and then enter your CSUSB login credentials.
