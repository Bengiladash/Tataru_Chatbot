https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=945906b304d3c1ea3173664d5a96cf68293c29093f Qualtrics Support Anyone with a Qualtrics account is able to submit a ticket through our support center. The steps to quickly accomplish this is below: Navigate to support.csusb.edu (TSC support center). Click Contact Support on the far right side of the page. Sign in using your Coyote ID and password. Select the option to "View All ITS Services" Select the "Troubleshoot" link under the Qualtrics section. Provide a detailed description of the issue you are experiencing. Click the Submit button to finish the form. By following this guideline, you can submit a support ticket so we may promptly help you resolve any issues you may be experiencing.
