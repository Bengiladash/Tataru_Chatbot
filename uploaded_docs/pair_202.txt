https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=9475ab92c00fcfe76dbc2244bb80f44afe6d8d3e3c How to Request a New PeopleSoft Query or an Update to an Existing Query Step #1: If requesting a new query, complete the form @: https://csusb.cherwellondemand.com/CherwellPortal/IT/One-Step/New%20Query If requesting an update to an existing query, complete the form @: https://csusb.cherwellondemand.com/CherwellPortal/IT/One-Step/Update%20Query Alternative Method, if direct links do not work: Step #1: Visit https://www.csusb.edu/its Step #2: On the right, click the “Open a Support Ticket” button to log into the Cherwell ticket system. (This requires duo authentication to request a ticket.) Step #3: From top banner, click the Service Catalog link. Step #4: Scroll down the list of Service Catalogs. Under Application Support there is a PeopleSoft category. Click the New Query or Update Query link to bring up the form to request a new query or update an existing query. Step #5: Fill out the form for your selected population criteria and expected query output fields (i.e., Coyote ID, names, email, etc.) and/or any existing queries for quick reference. Please allow at least 5 business days for the ACBI team to complete the request.
