https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=946d9350e98fc93ec40d5e41e195612ebebff687d1 How to convert a JPG to a PDF Step 1 Open your JPG and right click on the photo Step 2 Select the Print option Step 3 From the Printer drop down select Adobe PDF then proceed to print. (This option will convert your JPG to a PDF. Then save to your desired location)
