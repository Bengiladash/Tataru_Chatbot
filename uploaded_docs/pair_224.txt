https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=9469d25b414700d50a02d94054af87253c0d341a5d Cisco Finesse - Placing a call on hold and retrieving it Cisco Finesse Placing a call on hold and retrieve Ensure that you are in Ready state to receive incoming calls. When a call arrives at the desktop, click Answer. To place a call on hold, click Hold. To retrieve a hold call, click Retrieve. To end the call, click End. Your state changes to Ready and you are available for the next incoming call.
