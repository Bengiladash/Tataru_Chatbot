https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=94a04b22b11fe44da18ee54fcb820e42619495a82d Marking Card As Lost/Stolen To mark your card as Lost/Stolen follow these steps. 1. Log in to your Digital One Card Portal. 2. On the left blue plane under Account Management click the Mark Card as Stolen/Lost Button. This will deactivate your card. If you find your old card you have to reactivate it! To reactivate your card: 1. Navigate to the Mark Card as Lost/Stolen Button. it takes you to the info page. 2.Click the Activate Button.
