https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=947784a20c74a82c90186643e1b860141c900d4ecf What to do if you receive the Stale Request error in the MyCoyote App Note: This procedure applies only to the MyCoyote App Open the MyCoyote app on your mobile device In the upper righthand corner, click on the Silhouette icon Click on “Settings” Click on “Reset This App” Press “OK” This should reset the app and you should be able to login now
