https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=948cee63c4e43e93c3c319457fa227ac86b6be2c18 Set up Outlook for macOS Open Outlook From the Outlook menu, select Preferences Click on Accounts Click on Add Email Account Enter your email address in the format idnumber@csusb.edu Note that if you are asked, be sure to choose the Office365 email option, not the MS exchange option At the blue myCoyote login screen, enter your ID number and password If required, authenticate through DUO You will see a message that your account has been added. Press Done
