https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=9469d2257b43f3080efb7d40cd80e9e8f6e0da2d71 Cisco Finesse - How to Consult Transfer Cisco Finesse Consult transfer Ensure that you are in Ready state to receive incoming calls. When a call arrives at the desktop, click Answer. To consult with Supervisor or another user, click Consult. Enter Number in dial pad and click Call. To transfer a call, Click Transfer. Your state changes to Ready and you are available for the next incoming call.
