Student email accounts are Gmail accounts Access the Office365 Portal Login using an email address in the format idnumber@csusb.edu (note for students, you must omit “coyote” from the email address) Login to the CSUSB SSO page using your 9 digit Coyote ID and myCoyote password Authenticate via DUO You are now logged in to Office 365 online, you will see a list of recent files saved to your OneDrive as well as the Office apps on the left hand side
