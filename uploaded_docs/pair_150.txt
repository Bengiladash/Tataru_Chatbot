https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=945907c273b7f7dd86b69548bf8cac3f520bfb2b5e Outlook for Mac - Forward as Attachment From time to time a tech needs to see the full headers of a message to analyse the issue. The easiest method to do this is to forward the message as an attachment to the tech. To do this in Outlook for Mac, follow the steps below. Select the message. Make sure the Home tab is selected, then click the Attachment button. Compose the message the way you normally do and click the Send button when done.
