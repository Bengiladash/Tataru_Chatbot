https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=9469d2f2a789e533167cb34d43b616315281bcc363 Cisco Finesse - How to log out of Finesse How to log out of Cisco Finesse NOTE: Do NOT close your browser to sign out of the Cisco Finesse desktop. Finesse can take up to 120 seconds to detect that your browser closed and an additional 60 seconds to sign you out. Finesse may continue to route contacts to you during this time. From Cisco Finesse Desktop, click the status drop-down list and select Not Ready with the appropriate reason code. Click Sign Out. On the sign out confirmation screen, you can choose to exit the browser.
