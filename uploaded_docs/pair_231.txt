https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=946a49a5b20b18d73274f048bdad6ae2a1bdc58bf1 Cisco Jabber - How to set ring tones for multiple lines Cisco Jabber 12.5 Jabber | Set Ringtones for Multiple Phone Numbers Cisco Jabber lets you choose a ringtone to use for one, or for all your configured phone numbers, in a multiline environment. For example, you can set a unique ringtone for each of your office lines to personalize your call experience. Windows 1 Go to Settings > Options > Notifications . 2 In the Ringtone section, choose a ringtone. If you have multiple lines enabled for Cisco Jabber 12.8 or later, first select the line and then select the ringtone to play for that line. 3 Select Apply . MAC 1 Go to Preferences > Notifications. 2 In the Ringtone section, choose a ringtone. If you have multiple lines enabled for Cisco Jabber 12.8 or later, first select the line and then select the ringtone to play for that line. 3 To set a ringtone to all your numbers, go to Ringtone and choose a ringtone. 4 Select Apply .
