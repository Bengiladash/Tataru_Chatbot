https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=948fd28970ff6c32e0b8684eba8ecab7937f346ed0 DUO Managing your Devices Once you have enrolled in DUO mobile you can manage your Devices on Files: After authenticating with your Username and Password for MyCoyote and on the DUO authentication page, please click on "Need Help?" On the next screen please select "Manage Devices" On the next screen, you will be asked to verify your Identify through one of your Authentication Devices on file Once authenticated, you will be redirected to the site where you can manage your current devices 1.) This is where you can Edit the name of your current Device on file. 2.) This is where you can add another Mobile Smart Phone, as long as it is the same phone number that you already have on file. 3.) This is where you can add a new device with a different phone number to your account.
