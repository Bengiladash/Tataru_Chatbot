https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=9482cc5eef19d6e47f8ab54cd3b8369da9d63e48d5 Setting up an automatic reply in your @coyote.csusb.edu account Go to the myCoyote Portal Login using your id number and password Click on the student email tile Click on the Gear Icon and then See All Settings Under General, Scroll down to the "Vacation responder" section. Select Vacation responder on. Fill in the date range, subject, and message. Under your message, check the box if you only want your contacts to see your vacation reply. At the bottom of the page, click Save Changes.
