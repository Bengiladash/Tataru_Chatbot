https://www.csusb.edu/its/support/it-knowledge-base/detail?id=948f19da6220f408620b8f459499a903c3cc58c966 Uploading Grades Through Faculty Center (basic method) Navigate to the myCoyote Portal Login using your 9 digit ID number and password Authenticate through DUO Click on the Faculty Center Tile Click on the Grade Roster icon to the left of the desired course. Do not click on the icon that is located on the icon legend Within the grade roster, use the drop down menu to assign grades to each student. Be certain the grade roster type is set to “Final Grade” And Approval Status is set to “approved” At the bottom of the page, press Save You should see a notification that grades have been submitted
